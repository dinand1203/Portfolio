Internship Vunzige Deuntjes

Videographer & Editor for Urban Music Festival Brand

Role: Videographer & Editor Tools: Premiere Pro, After Effects, Blender Period: September 2023 - January 2024

Project Overview

Vunzige Deuntjes is an urban music and festival brand known for its hiphop, R&B and dancehall events in the Netherlands and Belgium. They organize over 100 events a year and host the Vunzige Deuntjes Festival in the Amsterdamse Bos, attracting tens of thousands of visitors.

During my internship at Vunzige Deuntjes, I worked as a videographer and video editor, creating content for social media and event promotion. I helped capture the atmosphere of club nights and festivals and turned this footage into short-form videos such as aftermovies, teasers and highlight clips for platforms like Instagram and TikTok.

The Challenge

The main goal of my internship was to strengthen the online visibility of Vunzige Deuntjes through consistent, high-quality video content. The challenge was to translate the energy of the events into short, engaging videos that work well on social media, fit the brand identity and help attract both new visitors and returning fans.

My Approach

I started by getting to know the brand identity and existing content style: the type of shots, pacing, colors and typography they use. During events, I focused on capturing crowd moments, artists, stage visuals and small details that tell the story of the night.

After each event, I selected the strongest footage, created an edit in Premiere Pro and added titles, overlays and subtle motion graphics in After Effects. I experimented with different formats (reels, stories, horizontal edits) so the content could be used across multiple platforms. Throughout the process I worked closely with the in-house team, incorporating feedback to make sure the videos aligned with the Vunzige Deuntjes tone of voice and campaign goals.

Results

The internship resulted in a series of finished video assets that were used on the Vunzige Deuntjes social media channels to promote events and build community engagement. The content helped give followers a clear impression of the festival and club experience and provided the team with a reusable visual style for future video content.

Several of the videos continued to be used after my internship as part of ongoing promotion, which shows that the material fit both the brand and long-term communication needs.
