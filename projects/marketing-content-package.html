Marketing Content Package

Social Media Content for Football Club

Role: Designer & Developer Tools: Blender, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects Year: 2023

Project Overview

This marketing content package was created for the social media channels of my football club. I've been playing football my whole life since I was six years old, and in 2020 I made my debut for the first team. As the club and the squad became more interested in increasing the club's online reach, a content package based on the players' shirts to present line-ups and man of the match announcements seemed like a fantastic idea. In this way, the club and the team are clearly visible on social media, and we hope this will help attract new members.

The Challenge

The challenge was to increase visibility on social media platforms with the aim of attracting new members and keeping existing members up to date with the weekly line-ups and man of the match announcements. This way, we also maintain interaction with the supporters.

My Approach

First, I had to create the 3D model of the football shirt. Once that was done, I used UV maps to apply the corresponding designs to the shirts. I then created the right composition and lighting and rendered a 10-second animation. After that, I imported these clips into After Effects to add the final touches, and then they were ready to be shared on Instagram.

Results

The results turned out very well, and the visuals are still being used today. Below you can see examples of the final content created for the club's social media channels.
